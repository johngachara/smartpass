KCA SMART PASS

Welcome to kca Smart pass. An alternative access control application for students.

If you do not have an account you must follow these guidelines for successful registration:

1. You must upload a copy of your school id and a photo of your face which will be used for activation of your account(All accounts remain inactive until activated by the application admin)

2. Only school email addresses are accepted in registration

3.Email identity is checked using a 6 digit code.

4. The device that is signing will by default be the only device allowed to login.
